Every Lender, lends money to a borrower, and expects the loan to be repaid with interest. This Loan is an asset in the books of the Lender. When for some reason the borrower stops the repayment and interest, the asset becomes a Non-Performing Asset or a Stressed Asset for the Lender. This is a challenge faced by all the lenders and the lender tries to manage the percentage of such assets to a manageable minimum. If he fails, his return on investment shrinks and eventually threatens the business to become a loss-making venture.

An NPA arises due to two main factors:

A willful default
A failure of borrower’s Business, due to various reasons.

Willful default occurs due to lack of financial integrity in the borrower. By lending to a Willful defaulter the Bank certainly walks into his trap. Willful defaulters can be controlled by strict regulatory laws. The preventive measures can be proper appraisal of the credit proposal, backed by huge amount of information from various agencies to rightly track the history of the borrower.

But Non-Willful defaulters are forced into default mainly due external economic factors like Recession, Change in Government Policies, Change in customer needs and moods, and innumerable other factors.

The Willful defaults should be handled by Negative Measures such as legal and punitive measures. But Non-Willful defaults should also be dealt with by Positive measures. The most important positive measure is to liquidate the security asset fast, appropriate the money for the loan account and release the borrowers as soon as possible, so that he can reorganize his life.

Unfortunately, in India, all defaulters are treated alike and there is lack of positive measures.

LAMP is an attempt at the positive measure. LAMP will not act punitive but supportive by introducing professionalism, skill and expertise, on the platform of today’s digital technological abilities and find a win-win-win solution to the Banker, Borrower and to the Buyer of the asset on lien.

LAMP is a special e-Market Place for non-performing assets – Lien Assets Market Place.
